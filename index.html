Making Your Way to Bhutan


Bhutan's serene landscapes and enriching culture await you. Here are your options to
enter the kingdom of happiness:


Flying to Bhutan: A quicker but costlier alternative, only two national carriers serve Bhutan -
Drukair and Bhutan Airlines. These airlines connect Bhutan with the world and offer you
a swift journey to start your magical experience.


Driving to Bhutan Border: If you're seeking a more affordable route, consider a road trip.
For those coming from India, you can fly to Bagdhora airport, then drive 4
hours to the border town of Jaigaon-Phuntsholing. It's another 4 hours drive from Phuntsholing
to Thimphu, Bhutan's capital. While it's a longer journey, it's an opportunity to witness
more stunning landscapes along the way. Remember, we recommend a minimum stay
of 4 nights to truly experience Bhutan's charm.
